Presentation Dance 2025

The Presentation Dance is one of the highlights of the Polmaise Bowling Club's year, providing an opportunity to celebrate the season's achievements and bring together members, friends, and supporters for an evening of entertainment, dancing, and camaraderie.

On the evening of 25 October 2025, members and guests enjoyed a wonderful celebration featuring presentations of trophies and awards, entertainment, dancing, and great company. The event showcased the club's vibrant community spirit and the bonds formed through the shared passion for lawn bowls.
