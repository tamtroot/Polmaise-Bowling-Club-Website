Polmaise BC Crowned Champions of the 2025 City of Stirling Top Fifteen!

Polmaise Bowling Club can proudly announce a major victory in the 2025 City of Stirling Top Fifteen tournament, securing the title with a thrilling final win on Sunday, 10 August 2025. This well-earned triumph reflects the club's skill, determination, and unwavering team spirit.

The Winning Journey

Round One (3 July 2025)

Polmaise opened their campaign with a decisive win over Doune Castle BC, advancing comfortably to the quarter-finals.

Quarter-Final (10 July 2025)

In a compelling match, Polmaise outplayed Bridge of Allan BC to book their place in the semi-finals.

Semi-Final (17 July 2025)

At home, Polmaise delivered a commanding performance against Cambusbarron BC to reach the final.

Final (10 August 2025)

Facing Causewayhead BC, Polmaise maintained their momentum and secured a decisive victory, clinching the championship.

This landmark victory showcases the dedication and talent of the Polmaise squad. From the tense opener through to the gripping final, they have demonstrated teamwork, precision, and remarkable composure under pressure.
