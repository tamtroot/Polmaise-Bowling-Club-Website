Membership

Join our community and enjoy the benefits of being a member

Benefits of Membership

Joining Polmaise Bowling Club offers numerous advantages for bowlers of all ages and skill levels.

Access to Facilities

Full use of our championship-standard bowling green and clubhouse facilities throughout the season.

Competition Entry

Eligibility to participate in all club competitions, leagues, and the opportunity to represent the club in external tournaments.

Coaching & Development

Access to expert coaching sessions and development programs suitable for all skill levels.

Social Events

Invitation to all club social events, including dinners, presentations, and special gatherings.

Club Governance

Voting rights at our Annual General Meeting and the opportunity to shape the club's future.

How to Join

Becoming a member of Polmaise Bowling Club is simple and straightforward.

1

Contact Us

Reach out by phone, or visit the club during opening hours to express your interest in joining.

2

Try a Session

We invite prospective members to attend a free introductory session to experience our facilities and meet current members.

3

Complete Application

Fill out our membership application form, which can be obtained from the clubhouse or downloaded below.

4

Pay Membership Fee

Submit your annual membership fee according to your membership category.

5

Receive Confirmation

Once your application is processed, you'll receive your membership card and welcome pack.

Membership Fees

Membership Category Annual Fee Details
Full Members (Male & Female) £80 Complete access to all club facilities and events for members aged 18 and over

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to have played bowls before to join?

No, we welcome complete beginners! Our club has experienced coaches who will help you learn the basics and develop your skills. We provide all necessary equipment for beginners.

Is there a dress code for playing?

For casual play and practice, comfortable clothing and flat-soled shoes are sufficient. For matches and competitions, white or club-approved clothing is required. Full details are provided in our welcome pack.

Can I try before I join?

Absolutely! We offer free taster sessions for prospective members. Contact us to arrange a convenient time to visit the club and try bowling under the guidance of our friendly members.

Are there opportunities for competitive play?

Yes, we offer various levels of competitive play, from friendly internal competitions to league matches and national tournaments. Members can participate at a level that suits their ability and ambition.

Do I need to own my own bowls?

While many members eventually purchase their own bowls, we have club sets available for beginners and those trying the sport. Our members can also advise you on purchasing suitable equipment when you're ready.
