Become a Sponsor

Partner with Polmaise Bowling Club and connect your business with our community while supporting local sport.

Benefits of Sponsoring Polmaise Bowling Club

We offer various sponsorship packages tailored to suit businesses of all sizes. By becoming a sponsor, you will:

Brand Visibility

Display your brand around our club, on team uniforms, and on our website, reaching hundreds of members and visitors.

Community Engagement

Connect with our diverse membership and demonstrate your commitment to supporting local community initiatives.

Networking Opportunities

Attend club events and meet potential clients, customers, and other business partners from the local area.

Corporate Social Responsibility

Demonstrate your business's commitment to supporting health, wellbeing, and community sport.

Exclusive Sponsorship Events

Invitation to sponsor-only events and annual recognition at our presentation dinner.

Media Coverage

Potential media exposure through our club's events and achievements featured in local and regional press.
