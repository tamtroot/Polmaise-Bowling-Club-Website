Thank You to Our Sponsors

Polmaise Bowling Club extends sincere gratitude to all our sponsors for their generous support. Their contributions help maintain our facilities, support our teams, and develop our community programs.

Primary Sponsors

Will Sweep

Excellence in Road & Street Cleansing Since 1999

Established in 1999 and owned by Steven Williams, managed by Ryan Neil, Will Sweep specializes in road and street cleansing, serving housing, construction, parks, severe weather emergencies, and road services sectors across Scotland. With an extensive fleet and highly trained operatives, services include snow clearing, street cleansing, roadside cleaning, drainage clearing, and area maintenance.

The company works closely with Edinburgh, Midlothian, East & West Lothian councils, offering contract and hourly-rate services. Will Sweep also operates a repair centre for commercial vehicles, fleet vans, HGVs, and construction machinery, ensuring high-quality maintenance and minimal downtime.

www.willsweep.com

Fallin Public House Society ("The Goth")

Supporting the Community Since 1911

Established in 1911, "The Goth" has been a longstanding supporter of Polmaise Bowling Club. It features a bar, lounge with TV for sports, darts and domino teams, and a function room for events.

Become a Sponsor

Partner with Polmaise Bowling Club and connect your business with our community while supporting local sport.

Benefits of Sponsoring Polmaise Bowling Club

We offer various sponsorship packages tailored to suit businesses of all sizes. By becoming a sponsor, you will:

Brand Visibility

Display your brand around our club, on team uniforms, and on our website, reaching hundreds of members and visitors.

Community Engagement

Connect with our diverse membership and demonstrate your commitment to supporting local community initiatives.

Networking Opportunities

Attend club events and meet potential clients, customers, and other business partners from the local area.

Corporate Social Responsibility

Demonstrate your business's commitment to supporting health, wellbeing, and community sport.

Exclusive Sponsorship Events

Invitation to sponsor-only events and annual recognition at our presentation dinner.

Media Coverage

Potential media exposure through our club's events and achievements featured in local and regional press.
