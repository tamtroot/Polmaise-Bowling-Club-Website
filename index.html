Welcome to Our Club

Established in 1911, Polmaise Bowling Club has been a cornerstone of the Fallin community for generations. With deep roots in the local mining heritage, our club offers a welcoming environment for bowlers of all skill levels.

Whether you're an experienced player or new to the sport, we invite you to join our friendly community and discover the joy of bowls in our beautiful facilities.
