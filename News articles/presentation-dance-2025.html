Join Us for a Fantastic Evening

Polmaise Bowling Club is delighted to announce our Annual Presentation Dance, which promises to be a fantastic evening to round off another great year at the club.

This year's dance has been organised by our Vice President, David Stewart, who is keen to see as many members, partners, and friends of the club join us for what we hope will become a long-standing highlight in the Polmaise calendar.

Entertainment & Refreshments

Jim Smith on Vocals

Get ready for a lively night of music and dancing with our fantastic entertainer!

Entertainment will be provided by Jim Smith on vocals, ensuring a lively night of music and dancing. A buffet is also included, making this excellent value for money and a perfect chance to celebrate the club's achievements together.

The evening will run from 8pm until 1am, giving everyone plenty of time to enjoy the music, food, and good company.
