Find Us

We're conveniently located in Fallin. Use the map below to find directions to our club.

Getting Here

By Car

Polmaise Bowling Club is located at Main Street, Fallin, Stirling, FK7 7HT.


From Stirling City Centre:
  • Take the A905 (Shore Road) east towards Fallin (approx. 3 miles).
  • Continue along Main Street; the club is on your left, next to The Goth.

From M9 Motorway:
  • Exit at Junction 9 towards Stirling Services.
  • Follow signs for A91 > A905 (Shore Road) towards Fallin.
  • Stay on Main Street, and the club will be on your left.

Public Transport

From Stirling City Centre:
  • Bus: Take the C69 or C68 bus service (First Scotland East) from Stirling Bus Station towards Fallin
  • Frequency: Approximately every 20 minutes during peak times
  • Journey Time: 10-15 minutes
  • Stop: Alight at Fallin, Main Street stop
  • Walking: Walk east along Main Street for approximately 150 meters; the club is next to The Goth public house
From Stirling Railway Station:
  • Exit the train station and walk to Stirling Bus Station (5-minute walk)
  • Then follow the bus directions above to reach Fallin

Parking

Limited spaces are available near the club. Additional parking may be available at:

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best times to visit the club?

The club is most active during weekday evenings and weekend afternoons. If you're interested in visiting, we recommend calling ahead to ensure someone is available to welcome you and show you around.

Can I hire the clubhouse for private events?

No, the clubhouse is too small to hold events. The pub next door "The Goth" has an events hall and is available for hire. Please contact the Goth for more information on availability and pricing.

How can I arrange a taster session?

Taster sessions can be arranged by contacting our membership secretary via email or phone. We typically run these sessions on Saturday mornings during the bowling season, but can accommodate other times by arrangement.
