Historic Achievements

For a complete record of our club's achievements from its founding, please contact the club secretary.

Key Historic Moments

Polmaise Bowling Club has a rich history of achievement dating back to 1914, including numerous Scottish Championships, international representatives, and local competition successes.

Our club archives contain records of all honours won since our founding, which are available for members to view.

The honours displayed on this page represent our most recent achievements (2010-2025).
